I research and develop interactive technologies that support people accessing devices, applications and services. My work is focused on improving access to computing technologies to people with varying degrees of ability, namely blind and motor impaired people, and on designing new interactions and workflows for pervasive healthcare, where I have focused mainly on neurodegenerative diseases (e.g., dementia). Alongside access and usability, I am concerned with how people are able to understand and secure their data, particularly from non-sophisticated insiders.
