My research is focused on improving access to computing technologies to people with varying degrees of ability, and on designing new interactions and workflows for pervasive healthcare. Alongside, I am concerned with how people, in general, are able to understand and secure their data, particularly from non-sophisticated insiders. I am an Assistant Professor at Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade de Lisboa and a researcher at LaSIGE.
